Here you can read the famous facts about the greatest athlete of all time.

  • Little Cassius was denied an autograph from his idol Sugar Robinson. When Ali became a famous fighter, he vowed never to refuse an autograph request. He never broke his promise throughout his career.
  • At the age of 22, Cassius Clay became the world heavyweight champion.
  • In 1967, Muhammad Ali refused to be drafted into the US military, saying: "I have no disagreement with these Viet Cong." A professional fighter was willing to serve time in prison for his pacifist ideals
  • The boxer's star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame is the only star that is placed on a wall and not on the ground, because Ali "did not want people to step on the sacred name of Muhammad"
  • In 1981, an eminent boxer talked a man out of committing suicide.
